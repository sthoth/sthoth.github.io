Some headline

This meeting brings together experts in stable homotpoy theory. The theme of the meeting is (loosely) around Galois theoretic aspects of stable homotpoy theory. There is also an introductory lecture, plus a research talk, by Noah Porcelli on Floer Homotopy Theory.
