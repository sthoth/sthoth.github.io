  • 15:0016:00 Constanze Roitzheim: Homotopy theory of finite total orders, trees and chicken feet

    A transfer system is a graph on a lattice satisfying certain restriction and composition properties. They were first studied on the lattice of subgroups of a finite group in order to examine equivariant homotopy commutativity, which then unlocked a wealth of links to combinatorial methods and other homotopy theory. On a finite total order [n], transfer systems can be used to classify different model category structures on [n]. The talk will involve plenty of examples and not assume any background knowledge.
